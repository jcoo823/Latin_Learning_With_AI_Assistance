## VOCABULARY BY THEME

### Family & Relationships
- pater (father), māter (mother)
- fīlius (son), fīlia (daughter)
- frāter (brother), soror (sister)
- amīcus (friend), hostis (enemy)
- vir (man), fēmina (woman)
- puer (boy), puella (girl)

### Government & Military
- rēx (king), rēgnum (kingdom)
- dux (leader), mīles (soldier)
- exercitus (army), bellum (war)
- pāx (peace), lēx (law)
- senātus (senate), cīvis (citizen)
- populus (people), urbs (city)

### Nature & Places
- terra (earth), mare (sea)
- mons (mountain), flūmen (river)
- ager (field), via (road)
- aqua (water), silva (forest)
- lūx (light), nox (night)
- aestās (summer), hiems (winter)

### Abstract Concepts
- amor (love), īra (anger)
- spēs (hope), fidēs (faith)
- tempus (time), diēs (day)
- vīta (life), mors (death)
- fortūna (fortune), gloria (glory)

### Actions (Verbs)
- amō (love), videō (see), audiō (hear)
- dīcō (say), legō (read), scrībō (write)
- dō (give), capiō (take), habeō (have)
- veniō (come), mittō (send), dūcō (lead)
- vincō (conquer), pugnō (fight), fugiō (flee)
