Anki Flashcards for Latin Learning

What are Anki Flashcards?

Anki is a powerful flashcard system that uses spaced repetition to help you learn and retain information more effectively. Instead of cramming, Anki shows you cards at optimal intervals based on how well you remember them, making your study sessions more efficient.
